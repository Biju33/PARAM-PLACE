Understanding Consciousness

Consciousness represents the awareness of internal and external existence. Despite millennia of analyses, definitions, explanations, and debates by philosophers and scientists, consciousness remains puzzling and controversial.

Levels of Consciousness

Consciousness is often described as having levels ranging from full awareness to deep sleep. Modern research suggests it's more of a spectrum than distinct states.

Collective Consciousness

The shared beliefs, ideas, and moral attitudes that operate as a unifying force within society. Many traditions suggest a global shift in collective consciousness is underway.
